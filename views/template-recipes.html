Matcha Green Tea Ice Cream

★★★★★

★★★★★
  • 5 h 30 m
  • 6 Servings
  • 330 Cal

This sweet treat is a healthier option for matcha ice cream. By replacing the whole milk and heavy whipping cream, we reduced the calories while keeping the creamy texture.

Ingredients

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk matcha to loosen. Add 1 Tbsp. milk and whisk until all lumps are removed. Whisk in the remaining milk and half-and-half.
  2. In a saucepan, combine the matcha/milk mixture, 3/4 c. light cream, and sugar. Cook over medium-low heat, stirring occasionally, until mixture begins to steam.
  3. In a bowl, blend cornstarch and remaining cream until all lumps are removed. Add to the saucepan, then cook, stirring, until the mixture begins to thicken and barely reaches a boil, about 5 minutes. Reduce heat to low immediately and stir for 5 minutes or until thick.
  4. Chill until cool, pour into an ice cream machine, and follow the manufacturer's instructions. For those using a machine with a built-in freezer, you can skip the cooling step and move right into pouring into the ice cream machine.
